Our Story

Since the 1950s

Founded in the 1950s by the Fong family, Kwong Yun Cheong Sauce Factory began its journey in Pasar Kuala Lumpur.

Through dedication and years of hard work, the company moved to a larger manufacturing facility in Kepong during the 1960s.

Today, the third generation of the Fong family continues the tradition of producing quality soy sauce using traditional brewing methods and carefully selected ingredients.

廣遠祥醬油廠由馮氏家族於1950年代創立， 早期於吉隆坡巴剎開始醬油製造與零售業務。

經過多年努力與發展，公司於1960年代遷至甲洞較大的製造廠房， 如今第三代家族成員繼續傳承傳統釀造工藝。
